Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Thiago Centurion Apps ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

This policy covers all current and future apps under the Thiago Centurion Apps brand. By using any of our apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

App Usage Information

We collect information about how you use our apps to improve functionality and user experience. This includes:

  • App features you interact with and how often
  • In-app events and actions you perform
  • Session duration and frequency
  • App settings and preferences

Device Information

To provide technical support and ensure compatibility, we collect:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (for analytics purposes only)
  • App version and build number
  • General location information (country/region level only, never precise location)

Purchase and Subscription Data

For apps with in-app purchases or subscriptions, we process:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Transaction identifiers (not payment card details)
  • Subscription renewal and cancellation events

Note: All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We never have access to your credit card or payment information.

Crash Reports and Diagnostics

When our apps crash or experience errors, we automatically collect diagnostic information to fix bugs and improve stability. This includes:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device state at time of crash
  • Error messages and codes

AI Feature Data (if applicable)

Some of our apps may include AI-powered features (such as image generation, text processing, or intelligent recommendations). For these features:

  • User inputs may be processed by third-party AI services
  • Generated outputs are stored locally on your device by default
  • We may collect anonymized usage metrics for feature improvement
  • Personal content is never used to train AI models without explicit consent

Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you contact support)
  • Precise location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photos, videos, or other media from your device (unless explicitly shared through app features)
  • Contacts, messages, or other personal data unrelated to app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs and crashes
  • Managing subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Sending important service notifications (e.g., subscription expiry)
  • Developing new features based on user behavior
  • Ensuring app security and preventing fraud

We do NOT use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our apps. These services may process your data on our behalf:

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data to validate transactions and manage subscription status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Mixpanel

We use Mixpanel for analytics to understand how users interact with our apps. Mixpanel collects anonymized usage data to help us improve app features. Mixpanel Privacy Policy

Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app crashes and errors. This helps us identify and fix stability issues quickly. Firebase Privacy Policy

Backend Services (if applicable)

Some apps may connect to our own backend services for features like cloud sync, data backup, or real-time updates. These services are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure and follow the same privacy principles outlined in this policy.

AI Service Providers (if applicable)

Apps with AI features may use third-party AI APIs (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar providers) to process your inputs. These services process data according to their own privacy policies, and we select providers with strong privacy commitments.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Crash reports: Retained until the issue is resolved, typically 90 days
  • Purchase data: Retained as required by tax and accounting laws
  • Account data (if applicable): Retained until account deletion is requested

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics by contacting support

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@thiagocenturion.com. We will respond within 30 days.

EU/EEA Users: Under GDPR, you have additional rights including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with reputable providers

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States and European Union, where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page
  • Notify you through the app or via email (if we have your email)
  • Post the updated policy on this website

Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: support@thiagocenturion.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
